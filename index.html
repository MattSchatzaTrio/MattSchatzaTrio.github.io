The Matt Schatza Trio is a funk, rock, folk and blues band rooted in the Minneapolis music scene. The Trio brings original live music to get your feet tapping and hands clapping while satisfying crowds with personally chosen cover favorites. The group was created by singer-songwriter and guitarist Matt Schatza, who takes his style from traditional blues and funk. Combining talents we have Adam Lain on the drums, who's styles and influences are inspired by a wide range of genres all the way from jazz to reggae and everything in between. Laying down bass is Sam Licari, who rounds out the trio with his funk rock background and influence. All together the M.S.T. Boys are a trifecta sure to not disappoint!
